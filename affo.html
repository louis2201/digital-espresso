Affogato

Affogato is a classic Italian dessert that combines the intense flavor of hot espresso with the smoothness of cold, creamy vanilla ice cream. The contrast of temperatures and textures creates a delightful indulgence for coffee and dessert lovers alike. A perfect ending to any meal – treat yourself to this sweet espresso experience!
