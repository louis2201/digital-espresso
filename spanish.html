Spanish Latte

Originating from Spain, the Spanish Latte combines the bold richness of espresso with the creamy sweetness of condensed milk, creating a balanced and indulgent experience. Topped with velvety steamed milk, this drink is perfect for those who enjoy a smooth yet slightly sweet coffee. Give it a try – your taste buds will thank you with every sip!
