Vietnamese Egg Coffee

A beloved specialty from Vietnam, Egg Coffee is a luxurious blend of strong, aromatic coffee topped with a frothy, rich mixture of egg yolk and sweetened condensed milk. The creamy texture of the egg foam complements the bold coffee, making it a truly unique treat. Discover the perfect balance of flavors – once you try it, you won’t look back!
